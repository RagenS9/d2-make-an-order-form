Puppy Party

Does your office space need some stress relief? Snuggling with animals is a proven way to reduce blood pressure and soothe a stressful environment. The puppies that come with a Puppy Party rental are still small, but weaned and ready to be adopted. They all have happy homes with foster parents until they find permanent homes, though, so don't worry about them--just snuggle and play! You can choose to host a "puppy" party with young, mature, or senior dogs. All have been socialized to be comfortable around new people and in group situations. If someone falls in love, please ask them to fill out an adoption application for consideration.

Direct party-goers to click on this link if they wish to be considered as a permanent fur-home to the dogs that attend your puppy party. All homes will be considered, but no home is a guaranteed match for any particular dog.
